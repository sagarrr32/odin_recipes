INGREDIENTS

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ginger
  • Tea leaves

RECIPE

  1. Add 3/4 cup water to a steel pot
  2. Put it over stove and add 1 teaspoon tealeaves and some grated ginger
  3. Let it boil for some time(2mins) and add 1.5 cup milk and 2 tblspoon sugar
  4. Let it boil enough (5 mins) and then turn the stove off
  5. Take two cups and serve the tea along with some biscuits and other food items
