POHA

INGREDIENTS

  • Poha
  • Water
  • Spices
  • Onions & Green Chillies

RECIPE

  1. Rinse Poha
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saute mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chillies
  3. Add chopped onions and after after they turn golden brown, add Poha
  4. Add turmeric powder and Salt
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and Lemon
